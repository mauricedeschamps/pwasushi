Common side dishes in sushi restaurants

Nigiri Sushi

Maguro (Tuna)

Lean red meat of tuna with mild flavor. A sushi classic.

Chutoro (Medium-fat Tuna)

Medium-fatty tuna with perfect balance of flavor and texture.

Otoro (Fatty Tuna)

The fattiest part of tuna with melt-in-your-mouth texture.

Salmon

Orange-colored with rich fat content. Mild and popular.

Kanpachi

Amberjack with firm texture and refreshing taste.

Hamachi (Yellowtail)

Rich and fatty with buttery texture.

Ika (Squid)

Chewy texture with subtle sweetness.

Amaebi (Sweet Shrimp)

Sweet flavor with soft texture. Often served raw.

Anago (Saltwater Eel)

Soft texture with sweet soy-based glaze.

Saba (Mackerel)

Rich flavor, usually served with vinegar cure.

Sanma (Pacific Saury)

Autumn delicacy with rich fat content.

Tako (Octopus)

Firm texture with mild ocean flavor.

Hotate (Scallop)

Sweet and buttery with delicate texture.

Hirame (Flounder)

Delicate flavor with slight chewiness.

Shima Aji (Striped Jack)

Premium fish with balanced fat and acidity.

Kinmedai (Golden Eye Snapper)

Red flesh with moderate fat content.

Kajiki (Swordfish)

Firm texture with mild flavor.

Awabi (Abalone)

Chewy texture with ocean aroma. Premium item.

Toro Salmon

Extra fatty cut of salmon belly.

Tuna Mayo

Canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise. Popular with kids.

Gunkan Sushi

Uni (Sea Urchin)

Rich, creamy texture with sweet ocean flavor. A delicacy.

Ikura (Salmon Roe)

Bursting texture with salty-sweet flavor. Usually soy-marinated.

Kani Miso (Crab Innards)

Rich and creamy crab innards with intense umami flavor.

Negitoro (Tuna Scallion)

Minced fatty tuna with green onions. Rich and flavorful.

Tuna Mayo

Canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise. Popular with kids.

Mentaiko (Spicy Cod Roe)

Spicy marinated cod roe with distinctive red color.

Natto

Fermented soybeans with sticky texture and strong flavor.

Yamaimo (Mountain Yam)

Slimy texture that aids digestion.

Okura Natto

Combination of natto and okra for extra sliminess.

Corn

Sweet corn, popular with children.

Hijiki

Nutritious seaweed with earthy flavor.

Kappa Maki (Cucumber Roll)

Refreshing cucumber roll. Named after a water spirit.

Avocado Roll

Kappa Maki (Cucumber Roll)

Natto Maki

Fermented soybean roll with sticky texture.

Takuan Maki (Pickled Radish)

Yellow pickled daikon radish with crunch.

Oshinko Maki

Assorted pickled vegetables in a roll.

Ebi Tempura Maki

Crispy shrimp tempura inside sushi roll.

Tuna Mayo Maki

Tuna and mayonnaise combination popular with kids.

Salad Roll

Lettuce and crab stick with mayonnaise.

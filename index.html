Sushi Essentials

Essential items for enjoying sushi

Nigiri Sushi

Maguro (Tuna)

Lean red meat of tuna with mild flavor. A sushi classic.

Gunkan Sushi

Tuna Mayo

Canned tuna mixed with mayonnaise. Popular with kids.

Sushi Essentials

Gari (Pickled Ginger)

Thinly sliced young ginger pickled in sweet vinegar. Served as a palate cleanser between different types of sushi.

Wasabi

Japanese horseradish with a sharp, pungent flavor. Used as a condiment for sushi. Real wasabi is rare and expensive - most is a mix of horseradish and food coloring.

Matcha (Green Tea)

Powdered green tea often served at sushi restaurants. Helps cleanse the palate and aids digestion. Has a slightly bitter, earthy flavor.
